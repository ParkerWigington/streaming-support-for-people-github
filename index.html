Support Ticket

Do you need help getting started streaming? You may already be streaming and just have a question, either way, we're here to help you. Here, you can submit a support ticket, and our tema will get back to you as quickly as possible with a solution.

Whether your question is about setting up your capture displays or how to retain more viewers, we can try our best to help you. With our team of experts, we've currently helped over 500 streamers with day-to-day support.
